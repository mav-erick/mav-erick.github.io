About

I am a product guy, and I specialize in:

  • Identifying the real problem.
  • Solving it in a creative, simple and efficient way.

The best products are characterized by their ability to solve a problem, and how it makes you feel. Hence I consider every aspect of a product equally important. I am not an advocate of any language or framework, I believe in using the best tool for the job.

After building several products single handedly, which included successes and failures alike, I have identified what it takes to make a product that solves a problem well. But I still have a long way to go in my quest of learning. In my free time, I like to write blogs that could help others with specific problems that I have faced during my journey. You won't find any articles about entrepreneurship or product management or anything along those lines, as I still consider myself a beginner in these areas and I have no business in telling other people what to do.

I developed a keen interest in programming when I was 11. Since then my passion and skills have constantly evolved to give me a very strong technological foundation. I did my bachelors at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, which is one of the best institutes in lndia for computer science and information technology. My university experience played a huge role in building the entrepreneurial skills in me, motivating me to start my own company.
